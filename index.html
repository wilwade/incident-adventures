Welcome to Incident Adventures

Incident Adventures is a tabletop roleplaying version of handling software incidents.

Immerse your team in simulated incidents to enhance collaboration, problem-solving, and communication skills.

How to Run Incident Adventures

Here are the steps to run Incident Adventures with your team:

  1. Prepare the scenario and roles for the incident.
  2. Gather your team and set the stage.
  3. Guide the team through the incident, encouraging collaboration and problem-solving.
  4. Debrief the team afterward to discuss what went well and areas for improvement.
